21 days to integrating Play into your creative practice:

"Play Your Way to Creativity: A 21-Day Challenge" is a program designed to help individuals tap into their creativity by incorporating play into their daily routine. The challenge involves committing to 21 days of playful activities that are intended to stimulate the imagination and inspire new ideas.

Get started today

Play is
important:

  1. First, play is a natural way for our brains to explore and experiment with new ideas and concepts. When we engage in playful activities, we are often more open to taking risks and trying new things, which can lead to innovative solutions and creative break throughs.
  2. Second, play can help us overcome creative blocks and mental barriers. When we approach a problem with a playful attitude, we are less likely to get stuck in rigid thinking patterns or feel constrained by self-imposed limitations.
  3. Third, play can also be a source of inspiration and motivation.

Overall, incorporating play into the creative process can help us tap into our imagination and explore new possibilities, leading to more innovative and fulfilling creative work.

How the challenge works:

  1. Sign-up by providing name and email address.
  2. Complete different activities everyday for 21 days in the given time and try to incorporate in your creative process.
  3. Share your learning process and how this is helping you journey with us at playyourway@google.com to get featured on our website.
