Day 21
The Expert

The Expert is about encouraging a mindset of openness that prioritizes possibilities over challenges. This activity is almost similar to the Bad Ideas exercise which is exploring ideas that might seem crazy or impossible. [1]

How it works

  1. Set the timer for 3 minutes by clicking the start button.
  2. Consider yourself as the "the expert" who will showcase your sales skills.
  3. Prepare a list of unrelated nouns that will be joined to form a new product. For example, "popcorn" and "milkshake," the product would be a "popcorn milkshake." or “Glitter” and “Tacos” the product would be a "Glitter Tacos.
  4. Now takes on the role of an enthusiastic salesperson and presents the benefits and features of the unique product you selected. It should emphasize its positive attributes, and why it would be a desirable and innovative item.

Importance

This challenging exercise encourages quick thinking and places your "expert" in the spotlight. The activity allows participants to adopt a playful mindset by embodying the role of an expert. This playful approach encourages individuals to let go of self-imposed limitations, explore new perspectives, and take risks in their creative thinking. This provides the freedom to experiment and showcase your skills in a playful and uninhibited way.
